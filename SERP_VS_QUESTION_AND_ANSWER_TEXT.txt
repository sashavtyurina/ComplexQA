ANSWER: Tough question :)

Best ideea (on my humble oppinion) is to avoid both horizontal and vertical scrolling. For horizontal is simple. Set the layout to a max of 770 pixels.
If you don't want vertical scrolling... it depends on viewer browser, how many extra toolbars he has, etc.
Typically for 800x600 resolution a 430 pixel heigth will do the trick.

As 800x600 is (still) used only by approx 30% of users you should think how your layout will look also at higher resolutions.
At 1024x768 it will still look ok. Higher than this it will be like a column in the middle of the page or on the left side, deppending on your choice of content positioning.

Try a self-adjusting approach. Use as much as possible CSS, avoid tables, keep content centered (both horizontal and vertical).
